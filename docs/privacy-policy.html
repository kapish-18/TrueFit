TrueFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

TL;DR: TrueFit is a fully offline app. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All your workout data lives exclusively on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. TrueFit does not collect any personal information whatsoever. The app:

  • Does not require an account, login, or registration
  • Does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifiers
  • Does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any device sensors
  • Does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs
  • Does not connect to any external server or API

2. Data Storage

All workout data (programs, exercises, sets, reps, weights, personal records, and settings) is stored locally in an SQLite database within the app's private sandbox on your device.

  • This data is not backed up to any cloud service by the app
  • This data is not accessible to other apps on your device
  • This data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app or use the in-app "Clear All Data" function

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone. Since no data is collected, there is nothing to share with third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.

The only way your data leaves the device is if you manually use the "Export Data" feature in Settings, which uses your device's native share sheet.

4. Third-Party Services

TrueFit does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data processing. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

TrueFit does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no data from any user, it is inherently compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

6. Data Security

Your workout data is protected by Android's application sandboxing, which prevents other apps from accessing TrueFit's data. The data is stored unencrypted on-device within this protected sandbox.

7. Your Rights

Since TrueFit stores all data locally and collects nothing remotely, you have full control over your data at all times:

  • View: All your data is visible within the app
  • Export: Use Settings → Export Data to get a full JSON export
  • Delete: Use Settings → Clear All Data, or simply uninstall the app

8. Changes to This Policy

If TrueFit ever introduces features that change how data is handled (such as cloud sync or analytics), this privacy policy will be updated accordingly before those features are released.

9. Open Source

TrueFit is open source software. You can review the complete source code, including all data handling logic, at: github.com/kapish-18/TrueFit

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer via GitHub: @kapish-18
